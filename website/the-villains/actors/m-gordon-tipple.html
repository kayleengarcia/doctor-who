Gordon Tipple

Gordon Tipple is a Canadian actor known for his roles in Time Runner, Doctor Who, and Leaving Normal. He also appeared in The X-Files and The Outer Limits. He became the fifth television actor to play the Master in the television movie Doctor Who.
